About BioWellth

Built by women, for women

At BioWellth, we believe women deserve a health system that actually sees them. Not a tracker. Not a search engine. A companion — one that connects the dots between your hormones, sleep, nutrition, emotions, and lifestyle so you can finally see the bigger picture of your health.

Why we built this

Women's health has been systematically under-researched and under-resourced. Symptoms get dismissed. Lab results get declared "normal" with no follow-up. Most wellness tools were built on data from men. BioWellth exists to change that — starting with Indian women who navigate a uniquely complex intersection of lifestyle, culture, and healthcare.

Holistic

We look at the full picture — not just one biomarker or one symptom, but the patterns across your whole life.

Personalised

Your body is not average. Our AI learns your unique rhythms and tailors every insight specifically to you.

Empowering

We give you knowledge and language so you can advocate for yourself in every health conversation.

Our mission

To make personalised, intelligent, women-first wellness accessible to every woman — so she can understand her body, trust her instincts, and take control of her health with clarity and confidence.

AI companion

Ask anything, any time

Your AI companion knows your health history, your cycle, your patterns. Ask it why you're tired at 3pm, what to eat in your luteal phase, or how to interpret a result.

  • Chat or voice interface
  • Context-aware, not generic
  • Remembers your history across sessions
Why am I so exhausted in the second half of my cycle?
Based on your data, your progesterone rises in the luteal phase and your sleep quality drops by ~18%. Combined with your iron being on the lower end, this creates a real energy dip...
What should I eat differently?
Focus on iron-rich foods with vitamin C — lentils, spinach, and a squeeze of lemon. Your body absorbs it better in this phase.
