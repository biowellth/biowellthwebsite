Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you sign up for early access, we collect your name, email address, city, and age range. If you upload blood test reports, we store those files securely. If you connect a wearable through Apple Health or Android Health Connect, we receive the health data you choose to share (sleep, HRV, cycle, activity). We also collect information you log directly, like symptoms and energy levels.

How we use it

Your data is used to provide functional interpretation of your blood work, power the AI companion, and generate gap analysis and test recommendations. We also use aggregated, anonymised data to improve our algorithms. We never use your personal health data for advertising or marketing.

Who we share it with

Nobody. We don't sell, rent, or share your personal health data with third parties. We don't share it with advertisers, data brokers, or anyone else. The only exception is if we're legally required to by Indian law.

How we protect it

Your reports and health data are encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is restricted to the systems that need it to provide your results. We don't store payment information (we use third-party payment processors).

Your rights

You can request a copy of all data we hold about you. You can ask us to delete everything at any time. You can withdraw consent for data processing. You can disconnect wearable integrations at any time. To exercise any of these rights, email support@mybiowellth.com.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep the site working. We use analytics to understand how people use the site. We don't use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update this page and notify you by email if the changes are significant.

Contact

Questions about your data? Email support@mybiowellth.com.
