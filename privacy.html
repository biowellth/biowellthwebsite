Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 April 2026  ·  Effective: 13 April 2026

Plain-language summary: We collect your email address when you join the waitlist. That's it for now. When the platform launches, we will collect health data you choose to share with us. We never sell your data, never share it with advertisers, and you can ask us to delete everything at any time. Your health data is yours.

1. Who we are

BioWellth India ("BioWellth", "we", "us", "our") is a health technology platform built for Indian women. We are currently in pre-launch and operating at biowellth.ai.

For the purposes of India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), BioWellth is the Data Fiduciary responsible for the personal data we collect.

You can contact us at any time at support@mybiowellth.com.

2. What data we collect and why

During pre-launch (current)

We collect your email address when you join our waitlist via Typeform. We use this to:

  • Send you early access updates and launch communications
  • Notify you when founding member spots open

You can unsubscribe from these emails at any time using the link in any email we send.

When the platform launches

When BioWellth opens, we will collect additional data that you actively choose to provide, including:

  • Account information — name, email address, date of birth
  • Health and biomarker data — blood test results you upload or share with us, functional interpretation outputs
  • Cycle and symptom data — menstrual cycle tracking, symptoms, energy, and mood logs
  • Wearable data — sleep, heart rate variability, activity, and skin temperature from connected devices
  • Lifestyle context — nutrition, stress, and other factors you choose to log

Health data is classified as sensitive personal data under the DPDP Act. We will only collect and process this data with your explicit, informed consent, which you can withdraw at any time.

Technical data collected automatically

Like most websites, we collect basic technical data when you visit, including your IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited. This is used solely to maintain and improve the site and is not linked to your identity.

3. How we use your data

We use your data only for the purposes we collected it for:

  • To provide the BioWellth service — generating functional interpretations, tracking trends, and personalising your AI companion
  • To communicate with you about your account, updates, and service changes
  • To improve the platform using aggregated, anonymised insights (never individual health data)
  • To comply with applicable laws

We do not use your health data for advertising. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not share your data with insurers, employers, or government bodies unless required by law.

4. Who we share data with

We share data only with service providers who help us deliver the platform, and only to the extent necessary:

  • Typeform — waitlist form collection (email only)
  • Cloud infrastructure providers — secure hosting and storage of your data
  • Laboratory partners — when you book an at-home blood collection, your name and collection address are shared with our accredited lab partner

All third-party processors are bound by data processing agreements and are prohibited from using your data for any purpose other than delivering services to us.

5. How we protect your data

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Health data is stored on servers located in India or within jurisdictions with equivalent data protection standards
  • Access to your data within our team is role-restricted and logged
  • We conduct regular security reviews

No system is perfectly secure. If we ever become aware of a breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you as required under the DPDP Act.

6. Your rights under the DPDP Act 2023

As a Data Principal under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a summary of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your personal data. We will action this within 30 days
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw consent for processing at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing
  • Grievance redressal — raise a complaint with us, which we will respond to within 30 days
  • Nominate — nominate another person to exercise your rights on your behalf in the event of your death or incapacity

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@mybiowellth.com with the subject line "Data Rights Request".

7. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active, or as long as needed to provide you with our services. If you ask us to delete your account, we will erase your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain certain records by law.

Waitlist email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe or the platform launches, after which you will be given the option to create a full account or have your data deleted.

8. Cookies

Our website currently uses only essential cookies required for the site to function. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy and seek your consent where required.

9. Children

BioWellth is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately at support@mybiowellth.com.

10. Medical disclaimer

BioWellth is a health information and wellness platform. We are not a medical provider, and nothing on the platform constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Functional interpretations are for informational purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making decisions about your health.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the platform evolves. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email (if we have your address) and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions or requests?

Email us at support@mybiowellth.com — we respond to all privacy-related queries within 5 business days. For formal data rights requests, use the subject line "Data Rights Request" and we will respond within 30 days as required under the DPDP Act.
